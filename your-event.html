Your Event

A practical guide to hiring the hall — everything you need to know before, during, and after.

Preparation

Before Your Event

Everything you need to organise ahead of the day so there are no surprises.

  • Book your date and confirm any extras you need (AV, microphones, children's furniture)
  • Receive our comprehensive Information Pack with everything you need to know
  • Plan your layout — we have 17 tables and 105 chairs to arrange as you wish
  • Arrange your catering — full kitchen available with cooker, dishwasher, urn, cutlery
  • Decoration rules: no tape, screws or nails on walls. Blu-Tack on the painted wooden board is fine. All decorations must be removed at the end
On the day

On the Day

A few things to know once you arrive so everything runs smoothly.

Dishwasher
Turn on the dishwasher on arrival — it needs 30 minutes to warm up before first use
Fire Exit Signs
Make sure all fire exit signs are illuminated when guests are in the building
Curtains
Use pull strings only to operate the curtains — never pull them across by hand
Heating
Heating will be pre-set for your event via HIVE — you can adjust the thermostat if needed
WiFi
Network: Chipperfield Village Hall Guest
Password: welcome
Closing up

Before You Leave

Please run through this checklist before locking up. It helps keep the hall in great condition for everyone.

  • Switch off dishwasher, urn, and cooker
  • Close and lock all windows
  • Switch off ALL lights including toilets and outside lights
  • Empty all waste bins into the large bins outside
  • Food waste and nappies must be taken away — not placed in any bins
  • Return key to key safe and scramble the lock
