Welcome

A charming venue on the village green

Nestled beside Chipperfield's beautiful common — with its cricket pitch, tree-lined lanes, and classic pubs — our village hall is a warm, welcoming space that brings the community together.

Whether you're planning a wedding reception, a children's birthday party, a weekly fitness class, or a village market, you'll find everything you need under one roof: a spacious main hall, fully equipped kitchen, bar area, AV system, and more.

We understand

Planning an event is stressful enough

You shouldn't have to worry about whether the venue has what you need, or spend your event running around instead of enjoying it.

Tell us about your event

Complete a short form with your date, event type, and any extras you need — children's furniture, AV equipment, microphones.

We'll confirm everything

We'll check availability, confirm your booking, and send you our comprehensive Information Pack so you know exactly what to expect.

Turn up and enjoy

The heating will be on, the hall will be ready, and you'll have everything from a commercial dishwasher to a sound system at your fingertips.

All included

Everything you need, nothing you don't

No hidden costs. Your hire includes all of the following.

Tables & Chairs
105 adult chairs, 17 tables, plus children's furniture on request
Full Kitchen
Cooker, commercial dishwasher, fridge freezer, hot water urn, cutlery
Bar Area
Fridges, sink, and external double door for easy stocking
AV System
Built-in projector, drop-down screen, HDMI and VGA connections
Sound & Microphones
Integrated speakers, cable and radio microphones, hearing loop
Lighting Options
Strip lights, spot downlighters, and revolving party ball with spots
Raised Stage
Stage with curtains — perfect for speeches, performances, and DJs
Heating On Arrival
HIVE-controlled central heating, pre-set for your event with adjustable thermostat
Free WiFi & Parking
Guest WiFi, adjacent parking, plus car park across the road
