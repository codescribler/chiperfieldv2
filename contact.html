Getting here

How to Find Us

Chipperfield Village Hall is located right beside the village common and green, in the heart of Chipperfield. As you approach the common, look for the hall's distinctive red front door — you can't miss it.

There is free parking directly adjacent to the hall, and additional parking is available in the car park across the road. The hall is well-served by local roads from Kings Langley, Hemel Hempstead, and the surrounding villages.

If you're using a satnav, the postcode is WD4 9BS. You can also use what3words: desire.famous.trades.
