Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about hiring Chipperfield Village Hall.

Your questions answered

Everything you need to know

Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch and we'll be happy to help.

Your hire includes everything you need to run a great event, all at no extra cost. You'll have access to 17 tables and 105 adult chairs, a fully equipped kitchen with a cooker (oven and hob), commercial dishwasher, fridge freezer, hot water urn, and a good supply of cutlery. There's a bar area with fridges and its own sink, an AV system with a built-in projector and drop-down screen, a sound system with integrated speakers and both cable and radio microphones, a hearing loop, flexible lighting options, HIVE-controlled heating, free WiFi, free parking adjacent to the hall, accessible toilets, and baby changing facilities. Everything is included in your hourly rate.

The hall can accommodate up to 180 guests standing, 120 seated in rows (theatre-style), or 100 seated at tables. We have 14 six-seater tables and 3 eight-seater tables, giving you plenty of flexibility for different layouts depending on your event.

Our standard hire rate is £32 per hour. When booking, remember to include time for setting up before your event and cleaning up afterwards. If you're planning an all-day event or a weekend evening hire, please get in touch and we'll be happy to discuss rates tailored to your needs.

Yes, bouncy castles are absolutely welcome! We know they're a highlight of any children's party. You're responsible for making sure the bouncy castle fits safely within the hall space and for supervising children while it's in use. Please check dimensions before you book your bouncy castle hire to ensure a good fit.

Yes, we do! We have 6 low tables (each seating 6 to 8 children) and 51 children's chairs, suitable for ages up to around 8-10 years old. These are available at no extra charge. Just let us know when you book so we can have them set out and ready for you.

Yes, the hall has a fully equipped kitchen available for all hirers. You'll find a cooker with oven and hob, a commercial dishwasher with automatic detergent dispensing (90-second and 180-second cycles), a hot water urn, a fridge freezer, and a good supply of cutlery and crockery. A couple of things to note: we don't supply sharp knives (please bring your own), and we don't provide tea towels, hand towels, or tablecloths, so do remember to bring those if you need them.

Yes, there's a built-in projector with a drop-down screen, perfect for presentations, slideshows, or film screenings. Both HDMI and VGA connections are available, and we have cables to connect phones, laptops, and DVD players. If you're not sure about compatibility, feel free to ask before your event and we'll make sure you're sorted.

Yes, the hall has an integrated sound system with speakers throughout, so music and announcements can be heard clearly everywhere. You'll also have access to both cable and radio microphones, which are great for speeches, presentations, or performances. There's a hearing loop installed as well, ensuring the hall is accessible for those who need it.

Yes, free WiFi is available for all hirers and their guests. Connect to the network "Chipperfield Village Hall Guest" using the password "welcome". It's great for streaming music, running presentations, or just keeping your guests connected.

There's free parking right next to the hall, which is the most convenient option for loading and unloading. For additional spaces, there's a car park directly across the road. Between the two, there's plenty of room for your guests.

You can decorate to your heart's content! The hall is your space for the duration of your hire, so feel free to dress it up however you like. The only thing we ask is that you don't use tape, screws, or nails on the walls, as these can cause damage. Blu-Tack on the painted wooden board is absolutely fine. All decorations must be taken down and removed by the end of your hire period.

Yes, baby changing facilities are available in the disabled toilet. It's spacious and accessible, so you'll have plenty of room.

The hall's heating is controlled via a HIVE system and will be set in advance for your event, so the hall will be warm and comfortable when you arrive. If you need to adjust the temperature during your hire, there's a thermostat in the hall that you can tweak up or down to suit your preference.

Food waste and nappies must be taken away with you at the end of your hire. These items cannot be left in any of the hall's bins. We'd recommend bringing a couple of bin bags specifically for food waste and nappies so they're easy to take home for disposal.

Before you leave, please run through this quick checklist: switch off the dishwasher, hot water urn, and cooker; close and lock all windows; switch off all lights, including in the toilets and outside; empty all waste bins (taking food waste and nappies with you); remove all decorations; and return the key to the key safe, making sure to scramble the lock code afterwards. It only takes a few minutes and keeps the hall in great shape for the next hirer.

Your safety is our priority. A defibrillator is available on the outside wall of the Two Brewers pub, just a few steps from the hall. First aid kits are located in both the kitchen and the bar area. The fire assembly point is the Two Brewers car park. If you need to reach the caretaker during your event, Chris Babey is available on 07498 055310. All of this information is also displayed in the hall.
