Executive Summary

Chipperfield Village Hall is a beautiful, well-equipped community venue with a loyal base of regular hirers. The current website has served the hall well since it was built — delivering a clear, cost-effective online presence for around £900. But as the committee's ambitions have grown, there's an opportunity to go further: surfacing more of the hall's information online, improving the booking calendar experience on mobile, and giving the committee better tools to manage enquiries.

This proposal outlines a modern, multi-page website. To show what's possible, we've already built a demo of the new homepage design — complete with navigation showing the proposed site structure — along with a working prototype of a booking management system. The goal is straightforward: make it easier for people to find the hall, understand what it offers, and book it.

What's already done: A new homepage design demonstrating the look, feel, and structure of the proposed site, plus a visual prototype of a booking admin dashboard. This isn't a pitch based on promises — it's a working demo you can try today.

Understanding Your Situation

The current website was built to be simple and cost-effective — and it's done that job well, giving the hall a solid online presence. As the committee's needs have grown, though, there are some natural opportunities to take things further:

Opportunities for Improvement

Area Opportunity
Booking calendar on mobile The embedded Google Calendar is difficult to use on phones — a purpose-built calendar would work much better
More information upfront A lot of useful detail (facilities, safety info, procedures) currently lives in the PDF — hirers would benefit from seeing this on the site
Google Calendar & committee access Team access and booking management have become difficult; a dedicated system would reduce friction
Booking journey A clearer path from browsing to enquiry — with optional extras and guided steps — could improve conversions
Additional content Details like the bouncy castle policy, children's furniture, hearing loop, and emergency info could be added to help hirers plan

What the Committee Wants

  • A fuller, more robust website that works well on all devices
  • Clear, up-to-date information drawn from the Hall Information Pack
  • A better booking experience for both hirers and the committee
  • Reduced admin burden — fewer repeat questions from hirers
  • A site that positions the hall as a modern, well-maintained venue

Recommended Site Structure

Based on the Hall Information Pack and the committee's goals, we'd recommend organising the new site into focused pages — each designed to answer a specific question a prospective hirer might have, and move them closer to booking.

⌂
Homepage
First impression — showcases the venue, highlights key features, and provides an at-a-glance availability calendar so hirers can immediately see if their date is free.
⚙
The Hall
Answers "will it work for my event?" — capacity breakdowns, table and chair counts, kitchen facilities, AV equipment, and practical details like WiFi and parking. All the information currently locked in the PDF, made browsable.
★
Your Event
Answers "what do I need to know?" — before, during, and after checklists for hirers. Emergency procedures, decoration rules, leaving responsibilities. Reduces repeat questions and sets clear expectations.
📅
Book Now
Converts interest into action — a guided enquiry form with pricing information, optional extras (AV, microphones, children's furniture), and a clear "what happens next" process so hirers know exactly what to expect.
?
FAQs
Catches remaining questions — expandable answers covering bouncy castles, parking, decorations, heating, food waste, and more. Every FAQ answered here is one fewer email or phone call for the committee.
📷
Gallery
Sells the space visually — a responsive image grid showing the hall set up for different events. Helps hirers picture their own occasion in the venue. Ready for updated photography.
📄
Documents
Self-service resource hub — downloadable Conditions of Hire, policies (H&S, Privacy, Safeguarding), and the Hall Information Pack. Organised by audience so hirers find what they need quickly.
📞
Contact & Location
Makes getting in touch easy — booking secretary, caretaker details, embedded map, what3words for emergencies, and directions with parking information.

This structure is a recommendation. The committee may wish to add, combine, or adjust pages — the final site architecture would be agreed together before build begins.

What We're Asking

We're not charging commercial rates for this project. CVH is a registered charity doing valuable work for the community, and this project also serves as a portfolio piece for Dreamfree. So rather than set a fixed price, we'd like the committee to choose what feels fair.
