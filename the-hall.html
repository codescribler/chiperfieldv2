The Hall

Everything you need to know about our fully equipped venue.

Overview

A spacious, versatile venue

The main hall offers 1,378 sq ft of flexible event space with a beautiful wooden floor and a vaulted ceiling with exposed beams, creating a warm and characterful setting for any occasion.

The versatile layout adapts to suit your needs, whether you are hosting a standing reception, a seated presentation, or a formal dinner:

  • 180 guests standing
  • 120 guests seated in rows
  • 100 guests seated at tables

Capacity

Tables, chairs & stage

Everything you need to seat and host your guests is included in the hire.

6-Seater Tables
14 rectangular tables measuring 156cm x 73cm, each seating 6 guests comfortably
8-Seater Tables
3 larger rectangular tables measuring 183cm x 76cm, each seating 8 guests, plus smaller tables available on the stage
Adult Chairs
Approximately 105 adult chairs for flexible seating arrangements across the hall
Children's Furniture
6 low tables (seating 6-8 children each) and 51 children's chairs suitable for ages up to 8-10 — available on request
Raised Stage
A raised stage with curtains, perfect for speeches, live music, DJs, performances, and as a backdrop for events
Facilities

Kitchen & Bar

The fully equipped kitchen has everything you need for catering your event, from a cooker with oven and hob to a commercial dishwasher with auto-detergent dispensing and both 90-second and 180-second wash cycles.

You will also find a hot water urn, fridge freezer, and a good supply of cutlery. Please note: no sharp knives are provided, so bring your own if needed. Tea towels, hand towels, and tablecloths are not supplied.

The separate bar area includes a fridge, sink, and an external double door for easy stocking of drinks and supplies before and during your event.
